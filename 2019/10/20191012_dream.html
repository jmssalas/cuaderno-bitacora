DREAM

"I could see she is happy with him", Octubre 12, 2019

I had a dream last night. She was there. And he too. It was the first time in two and an half years that he appears. Surprisingly, I really felt comfortable, with them, with the situation.

I have wanted to meet him for long time. Really. It may be a little weird, because he was the reason she made the decision. We know she was right and she made the right decision. Now, I do not really know how I felt about him at that moment. He did not do anything although he was the reason.

And now, that dream. Usually, I do not remember my dreams, and I do not know because this dream yes and I do not know what can mean. They seem very happy together and in her eyes, I could see she is happy with him. And that makes me happy.

But, it was a dream.
